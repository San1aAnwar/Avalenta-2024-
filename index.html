About Avalenta

"Avalenta" stands as the highly anticipated Intercollegiate Cultural Fest held on May 20th, 2024. Hosted by St. Anne’s First Grade College for Women, located on Millers Road, this event epitomizes a surge of diverse talents. The coined term "Avalenta" symbolizes an abundant wave of cultural prowess and artistic flair. It serves as a platform for students to exhibit their unique cultural heritage, artistic abilities, and innovative ventures. This year's edition guarantees a thrilling lineup of twelve events, encompassing both on-stage and off-stage activities. We eagerly anticipate your presence at this celebration of talent and creativity.
